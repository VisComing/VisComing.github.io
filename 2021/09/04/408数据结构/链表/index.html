双链表

双链表一般会有左右两个哨兵head和tail，两个哨兵指向不同的结点

head->next = tail, tail->prev = head;//初始化双链表

p->next = head->next; p->prev = head; head->next->prev = p; head->next = p;//在head后插入一个元素p

p->prev->next = p->next; p->next->prev = p->prev;//删除元素p
